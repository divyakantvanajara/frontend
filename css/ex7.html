Getting Started

We at Team Genesis would like to welcome you to “The World”. We've compiled and written this guide to help make your first steps into our world as painless as possible. Unfortunately .hack//fragment does a poor job at explaining the nuances that makes the game tick. Combine that with the lack of resources and the fact that it was never released officially in the west. Well it can be pretty daunting for newcomers. Sure if you had prior experience with other titles you might have enough under your belt to hit the ground running in Fragment. But for the person who has zero experience gained in “The World” you might be at a loss. So that's where we come in! Lore “The World” is under attack by monsters, which has forced the forces of good to retreat into fiercely defended cities and forts. Only adventurers are allowed to penetrate the magical shields around these safe havens and battle with the monsters that lurk outside. These adventurers draw their power through their mastery of waves, symbols that are inscribed onto their bodies and equipment that allow them to channel the power of the elements into their bodies in the form of magical skills and abilities.

character Creation

Fragment has a pretty basic character creation with a number of preset models from both the anime and the games. It's tied into the class system so mixing and match models isn't a thing. Luckily there's something for everyone and almost all the models are fantastic! Players can choose their name, the type of class they want to play and their choice between a number of premade body types. With large, medium, and small, 3 heights and several preset color schemes.

Long Arm

Long Arms are easily spotted by the weapon of choice the polearm and are one of physically strong class in “The World”. Long Arms and Twin Blades are near equals but differ in that they have greater attack and defense stats as well as a superior attack range. This comes with the sacrifice of magical and multi-hit skills. Long Arms also share the same HP and SP growth rates as Heavy Blades and Blademasters.
